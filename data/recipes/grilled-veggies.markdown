# Grilled Veggies

A flexible approach to grilling mixed vegetables.

## Ingredients

- Assorted vegetables (see suggestions below)
- Olive oil or Italian dressing
- Salt
- Pepper

### Vegetable Suggestions

- Cherry tomatoes
- Onion quarters
- Bell peppers (any color)
- Zucchini
- Yellow squash
- Pineapple chunks
- Whole garlic cloves

## Directions

1. Cut vegetables into similar-sized pieces so they cook evenly. Leave cherry tomatoes whole.
2. Toss vegetables with olive oil (or Italian dressing), salt, and pepper.
3. Place in a grill pan with holes and grill over medium-high heat.
4. Toss occasionally until vegetables are charred and tender.

## Notes

- A grill pan with holes is the best container - keeps small pieces from falling through while allowing smoke and char.
- Italian dressing works well as a marinade and adds extra flavor.
- Pairs well with grilled corn or grilled chicken.

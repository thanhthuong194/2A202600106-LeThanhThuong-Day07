# Beef Kofte

Based on [The Fresh 20](https://thefresh20.com/)

## Ingredients

- 1½ lb lean ground beef (90% lean)
- ⅓ cup fresh mint, minced
- ¼ cup grated yellow onion
- 3 garlic cloves, minced
- 2 Tbsp paprika
- 2 tsp ground cumin
- 1 tsp kosher salt
- ½ tsp black pepper
- ¼ tsp cayenne pepper

## Directions

1. Mix beef with all other ingredients in a large bowl.
2. With moistened hands, roll into oblong shapes (about 2 Tbsp each).
3. Grill over medium-high heat about 15 minutes, turning every 3 minutes. Or bake at 425°F for 10-12 minutes until no longer pink in center.

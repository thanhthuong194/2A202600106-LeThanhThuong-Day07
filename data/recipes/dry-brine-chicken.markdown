# Dry Brine Chicken

Based on [The Fresh 20](https://thefresh20.com/)

## Ingredients

- 1 Tbsp kosher salt
- 1 tsp Herbes de Provence
- 1½ tsp fresh dill, chopped
- ½ tsp black pepper
- 4-6 bone-in, skin-on chicken breasts (~3 lbs)

## Directions

1. Combine salt, herbs, dill, and pepper. Sprinkle on both sides of chicken.
2. Refrigerate at least 3 hours or overnight (up to 24 hours).
3. Bake at 375°F for 30-40 minutes until no longer pink in center.

# Avocado Toast

## Ingredients

- bread
- avocados
- salt
- pepper
- garlic (optional)
- lemon juice (optional)
- red pepper flakes (optional)
- egg (optional)

## Directions

1. Toast bread.
2. Mash avocados with salt, pepper, and any optional seasonings.
3. Spread on toast.
4. Top with a fried or poached egg if desired.

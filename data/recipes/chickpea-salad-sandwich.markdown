# Chickpea Salad Sandwich

Based on [Serious Eats 15-Minute Salad is Half Falafel, Half Hummus](https://www.seriouseats.com/15-minute-falafel-hummus-salad-recipe)

A quick chickpea salad with tahini dressing - like falafel meets hummus.

## Ingredients

### Salad

- 1/3 cup tahini paste, stirred well before measuring
- 1/4 cup lemon juice (from 2 lemons)
- 2 tablespoons mayonnaise
- 1 clove garlic, chopped
- 1 teaspoon Diamond Crystal kosher salt
- 3/4 teaspoon ground cumin
- 1/4 teaspoon ground coriander
- 2 tablespoons water
- 2 (15 oz) cans chickpeas, drained and rinsed
- 2 Persian cucumbers or 1/2 English cucumber, cut into 1-inch chunks
- 1/2 cup picked fresh parsley, mint, or cilantro (or combination)

### For Serving

- Hearty bread or pita pockets
- Sliced or chopped tomatoes (optional)
- Romaine or butter lettuce (optional)
- Pickled red onions, turnips, or mixed vegetables (optional)
- Zhug (optional)

## Directions

1. In food processor, combine tahini, lemon juice, mayonnaise, garlic, salt, cumin, and coriander. Process until combined, about 10 seconds. With processor running, slowly pour water through feed tube until smooth emulsified sauce forms, about 10 seconds.
2. Add herbs, chickpeas, and cucumbers. Pulse 5-7 times until well combined and broken down but still chunky. Season with salt if needed.
3. Stuff about 1/2 cup salad into pita pocket or onto bread. Top with tomatoes, lettuce, pickled vegetables, and/or zhug.

## Notes

- Serves 4-6. Takes 15 minutes.
- Tahini separates - stir well before measuring.
- Celery can substitute for cucumber.

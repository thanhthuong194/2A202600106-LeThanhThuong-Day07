# Fish Tacos

Based on [Spend With Pennies Easy Fish Tacos](https://www.spendwithpennies.com/fish-tacos-recipe-onion-slaw/)

## Ingredients

### Fish

- 1.5 lbs tilapia or cod/haddock fillets
- 1 tablespoon olive oil

### Spice Rub

- 1 tablespoon chili powder
- 1/2 teaspoon cumin
- 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
- 1 teaspoon paprika
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1/2 teaspoon pepper
- 1 teaspoon oregano
- 1/2 teaspoon onion powder

### Tacos

- 8 (6-inch) corn or flour tortillas
- 1 sliced avocado
- 1 lime, wedged
- Toppings to taste

### Fish Taco Sauce (optional)

- 2 tablespoons mayonnaise
- 3 tablespoons sour cream
- 1/2 lime, juiced
- 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
- 1/2 teaspoon cumin
- 1/2 teaspoon sriracha (or to taste)

## Directions

1. Preheat oven to 400°F.
2. Combine all fish taco sauce ingredients and set aside.
3. Mix spice rub ingredients. Rub into fish fillets.
4. Place fish on parchment-lined pan, drizzle with olive oil.
5. Bake 12-15 minutes until flaky and cooked.
6. Heat tortillas according to package.
7. Break fish into chunks and divide between tortillas. Top as desired.

## Cabbage Onion Slaw

- 1 cup green cabbage, shredded
- 1 white onion, thinly sliced
- 2 tablespoons cilantro, chopped
- 1/3 cup sour cream
- 2 tablespoons fresh lime juice
- 1 jalapeño pepper, seeded and chopped

## Notes

- Serves 4. Total time 25 minutes.
- A bit of work but tasty. The sauce and slaw can be prepped ahead of cooking the fish.

# Eggs in Spicy Minted Tomato Sauce

Based on [Food52 Eggs in Spicy Minted Tomato Sauce](https://food52.com/recipes/22806-eggs-in-spicy-minted-tomato-sauce)

A riff on shakshuka with mint.

## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 1 medium yellow onion, diced
- 3-4 garlic cloves, minced
- 1 jalapeño pepper, seeded and minced
- 1 (28 oz) can crushed tomatoes
- Sriracha or hot sauce to taste
- 1 bay leaf
- 1 tablespoon chopped fresh mint
- Salt and pepper
- 4 large eggs
- Crusty bread, for serving

## Directions

1. Heat butter and olive oil in a large deep skillet over medium heat. Add onion and sauté about 5 minutes until translucent. Add garlic and jalapeño, sauté 1 more minute.
2. Add crushed tomatoes, hot sauce, and bay leaf. Bring to boil, then reduce to steady simmer. Simmer uncovered 20-30 minutes until sauce has thickened. Stir in mint.
3. Make four depressions in sauce. Crack an egg into each. Season with salt and pepper. Cover and cook until whites are set and yolks are done to your liking.
4. Serve from pan with crusty bread for mopping.

## Notes

- Serves 2 as main, 4 as appetizer.
- Cook yolks just barely set so they're slightly runny but don't dilute sauce.

## My Notes

- Good for reusing extra tomatoes or mint.

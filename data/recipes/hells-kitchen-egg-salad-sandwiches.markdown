# Hell's Kitchen Egg Salad Sandwiches

[Original recipe](http://sherrissanity.blogspot.com/2012/02/hells-kitchen-knock-off.html)

## Ingredients

### Egg Salad

- 8 eggs
- 1/4 C diced green onions (chives)
- 1/4 tsp celery salt
- 1/8 tsp pepper
- 1 tsp fresh lemon juice
- 1/2 tsp cajun seasoning (McCormick's is good - not too salty)
- about 1/4 C mayonnaise

### Sandwich

- croissants or thickly sliced sourdough
- bacon
- avocado
- lettuce

## Directions

1. Bring eggs to a boil, remove from heat, cover and let sit about 10 minutes.
2. Immediately run under cool water to stop cooking. Peel and chill in fridge for at least 2 hours.
3. Mash eggs with a potato masher or pastry blender until desired chunkiness.
4. Add green onions, celery salt, pepper, lemon juice, cajun seasoning, and mayo. Mix well.
5. Serve on croissants or sourdough with bacon, avocado, and lettuce.

## Notes

If you don't have cajun seasoning, hot sauce and/or chili powder and paprika work as substitutes.

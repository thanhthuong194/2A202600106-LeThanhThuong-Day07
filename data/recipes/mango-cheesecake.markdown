# Mango Cheesecake (No-Bake)

Based on [RecipeTin Eats No Bake Mango Cheesecake](https://www.recipetineats.com/no-bake-mango-cheesecake/)

No-bake cheesecake with mango filling and mango jelly topping.

## Ingredients

- 150g graham crackers + 100g butter + 2 tbsp sugar (base)
- 3 large ripe mangoes
- 4½ tsp + ¾ tsp gelatin powder
- 500g cream cheese
- ⅔ cup sugar
- 300 ml whipping cream
- 1 tbsp lemon juice

## Directions

1. Blitz crackers with melted butter and sugar. Press into 9" springform pan.
2. Bloom 4½ tsp gelatin in ½ cup water, microwave to dissolve.
3. Blitz 2 mangoes until smooth. Reserve ¾ cup puree for topping.
4. Add cream cheese, cream, sugar, and gelatin to processor. Blitz until smooth. Pour into pan, refrigerate 3+ hours.
5. Make jelly: bloom ¾ tsp gelatin in ¼ cup water, dissolve, whisk with reserved puree and lemon juice. Pour over filling.
6. Refrigerate 12+ hours. Top with whipped cream and mango cubes if desired.

## Notes

- My mother-in-law makes this and my daughter loves it.
- Use ripe mangoes for best results.

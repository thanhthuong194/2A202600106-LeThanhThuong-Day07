# Buffalo Chicken Mac and Cheese

[Inspiration](https://www.bettycrocker.com/recipes/buffalo-chicken-mac-and-cheese/d5a9fd21-a136-4b43-baaf-b7ed79b6b38b)

## Ingredients

### Pasta

- macaroni
- cream cheese
- cheddar cheese
- gorgonzola
- celery salt
- ranch powder

### Chicken

- rotisserie chicken, shredded
- buffalo sauce (see [buffalo chicken sandwiches](./buffalo-chicken-sandwiches.markdown) for sauce ideas)
- red onions
- spices

### Topping

- fresh minced celery

## Directions

1. Cook macaroni according to package directions.
2. Toss chicken with buffalo sauce and let it soak.
3. Make cheese sauce with cream cheese, cheddar, gorgonzola, celery salt, and ranch powder - keep it "cool" flavored.
4. Combine pasta with cheese sauce.
5. Add the "hot" buffalo chicken.
6. Top with fresh minced celery.

## Notes

The contrast of "cool" cheese sauce and "hot" spiced chicken is the key.

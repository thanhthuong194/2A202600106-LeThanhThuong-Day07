# Lemon Rice Soup

A bright, comforting chicken soup with lemon and dill.

## Ingredients

- 6-8 cups chicken broth
- 1-2 cups shredded or chopped chicken
- 1 cup rice
- 2 carrots, diced
- 2 stalks celery, diced
- 1 onion, diced
- Juice of 1-2 lemons
- Fresh dill, chopped
- Salt and pepper to taste
- 2 egg yolks (optional, for avgolemono-style)

## Directions

1. Bring chicken broth to a boil in a large pot.
2. Add onion, carrots, and celery. Simmer until vegetables begin to soften, about 10 minutes.
3. Add rice and cook until tender, about 15-20 minutes.
4. Stir in lemon juice and dill. Season with salt and pepper.
5. Serve hot.

### Avgolemono Variation

For a richer, creamier soup:

1. Whisk egg yolks with lemon juice in a bowl.
2. Slowly ladle about 1 cup of hot broth into the egg mixture, whisking constantly to temper.
3. Pour the tempered egg mixture back into the pot, stirring. Do not boil after adding eggs.

## Notes

- Great way to use a chicken carcass - simmer it to make the broth and pick the meat off.
- Adjust lemon to taste - start with one lemon and add more as needed.
